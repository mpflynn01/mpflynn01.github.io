Welcome to Flynn Fest

Flynn Fest gets its origins from the archaic decade of the 90s where John and Darlene Flynn threw an annual summer party that brought friends and family together from all over the country.

After an almost ten year hiatus, we are bringing the party back with tons of live music, great food, games, and high fiving.

So come celebrate with us this summer.
