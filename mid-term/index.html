About Four Flynns

Four Flynns Brewing Co. started as a passion project by John Flynn and now is a full fledged operation based out of Bolingbrook, IL. What was once a nuclear family concoting in the kitchen has grown into an operation revered regionally. You can find FFBC's items at many of your favorite bars and grocery store chains around the midwest. Or come to our tasting room open on the weekends.
